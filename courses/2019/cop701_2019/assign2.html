COP701 Assignment 2

Zaxxon Game

Problem statement

Implement the Zaxxon game: Youtube link (1:03)

Use Unity3D or the Unreal engine.

1. Make it a standalone application.

2. Incorporate very nice visual elements.   

3. Make the graphics very smooth.

4. Gradually make a level harder.

5. Have at least five levels. Be creative and improvise. Add at least two new features.



Marking Scheme

Feature Marks (100)
Five levels and at least two layouts 25
Scoring, fuel indicator, number of lives indicator, height indicator 10
Obstacles: tanks, enemy planes, walls 20
Movement of plane: left-right up-down 10
Overall graphics 25
Report and coding style 10
Maximum bonus 25
