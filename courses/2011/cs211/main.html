26/08/2011 IMPORTANT Regarding Homework 2 - You are not allowed to change the algorithm of both lcs and quicksort. The spirit of the homework is to teach you assembly language programming, and to bring in a spirit of healthy innocuous competition. Let us not violate that. Minimal modifications to the operation of the algorithm are fine. However,replacing quicksort with bubblesort or any other algorithm will lead to disqualification.

The 'Computer Architecture' course is intended to teach undergraduate students in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science the basics of computer architectures.

Credits :  5   [3-1-2] (L-T-P)

Prerequisites:

 CSL101/CSL102 , CSL201 & (EEL201 & EEP201) (concurrent registration in EEL201 & EEP201 is acceptable)

TextBook:

Computer Organization and Design, 4th Edition  The Hardware/Software Interface  "ARM EDITION"

By David A. Patterson  &  John L. Hennessy
