28 NOVEMBER 2017, IIT DELHI, INDIA

One-day Workshop on Architecture Implication of Security in IoT Processors

This event is a one day workshop highlighting the key challenges in worldwide application of IoT devices. Problems encountered while scaling to authentication and security to power consumption of IoT devices will be the major topic of discussion in the workshop.
