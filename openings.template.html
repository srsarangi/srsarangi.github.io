I get a lot of e-mail from students for different kinds of positions. It is often not possible to judge the merit of a candidate from his/her resume. Unless the candidate has something outstanding in his/her resume such as graduation from a very top institute with a great CGPA, or superlative performance in a standardized test such as GATE, it is very difficult for me to judge. In that situation, I will ask him/her to read my book on computer architecture (details can be found here) and schedule an interview on phone.

Students

I am currently looking for motivated Ph.D, M.Tech, and M.S students. Ph.D and M.S research students are provided with excellent facilities in IIT Delhi. They include hostel facilities, office space, computing facilities, and support for attending international conferences. They also get to engage in high end research, interact with reputed researchers from other universities, and even spend time at research labs, or foreign universities. Secondly, most Ph.D/M.S students publish papers in top international conferences and journals, and typically have a successful career in high end R&D facilities, academia, or industrial research labs. PLEASE NOTE: It is possible to be employed in a research project position, and simultaneously pursue a Ph.D or M.S (research).

Please see the projects and research pages for the details of some of the current projects that we are involved in.
