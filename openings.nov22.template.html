Project Positions

We are urgently looking to fill in three research project positions. The projects are scheduled to begin by the end of this year. We are looking for candidates who preferably have an M.Tech or equivalent degree in computer science or electronics engineering, and would like to pursue a research career. Good programming skills, strong analytical skills, and knowledge of fundamentals in computer architecture are necessary. Exceptional candidates with a B.Tech or equivalent degree can also apply.

In the first project, a candidate will be expected to work on a state of the art architecture simulator and implement a couple of research ideas (see the flyer here). In the second project, the candidate will be working on power and temperature management algorithms (see flyer here). There is a good possibility of getting research papers, and patents from these projects. Secondly, it is possible for the candidate to simultaneously pursue a higher degree in IIT. However, the candidate needs to go through the relevant admission process, which has its own independent selection procedure and publicly advertised shortlisting criteria.

Please track this page to find a formal announcement along with interview dates at a future point of time. In the meantime, you can send me your resume.

Students

I am currently looking for motivated Ph.D, M.Tech, and M.S students. Ph.D and M.S research students are provided with excellent facilities in IIT Delhi. They include hostel facilities, office space, computing facilities, and support for attending international conferences. They also get to engage in high end research, interact with reputed researchers from other universities, and even spend time at research labs, or foreign universities. Secondly, most Ph.D/M.S students publish papers in top international conferences and journals, and typically have a successful career in high end R&D facilities, academia, or industrial research labs. PLEASE NOTE: It is possible to be employed in a research project position, and simultaneously pursue a Ph.D or M.S (research).

Please see the projects page for the details of some of the current projects that we are involved in. Some of my students are currently focused on hybrid computer architectures, nano-scale interconnects, fault tolerance, and large scale parallel simulation. However, I am also open to any other idea in computer architecture that students might be willing to pursue. If you are academically outstanding and want to pursue a career in research or advanced R&D then please send me your resume.
