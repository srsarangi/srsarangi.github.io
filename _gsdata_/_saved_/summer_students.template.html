information for summer students



I believe that if executed properly, a summer internship can add a lot of value to a resume. In general, people prefer to do summer internships in industry to get a feel of the real world. Summer internships in academic institutions are primarily to get a flavor of research. Please note that the number of summer internship positions will tend to vary year by year, and is dependent on the policies of the institute and department. Furthermore, we need to have enough seats, computers, and other logistics for the interns. Please note that funding and availability of positions is not guaranteed. Lastly, please note that students from IIT Delhi, will be my first priority. Here are some more of my thoughts regarding this issue.

  •  Students from IIT Delhi - First priority. Please meet me in my office to discuss anything specific that you would like to be involved in.
  •  From other academic institutions - I would mostly prefer third year or M.Tech first year students. Secondly, it would be nice if you could apply through a faculty member.  Please ask him to write a few lines about you.
