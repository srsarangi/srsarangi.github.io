short term courses/ summer courses


If any organization wants to invite me for teaching a short term course in India or outside India, then please initiate a conversation with me. If the specific course is within my area of expertise, fits my schedule, and is as per IIT Delhi rules, then I would be happy to conduct the course. I would advise organizations to contact me well in advance such that we have sufficient time to plan the course, and get all necessary approvals from IIT Delhi.
