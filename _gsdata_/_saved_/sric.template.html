sponsored research and industrial consultancy


IIT Delhi allows its faculty to engage in sponsored research and consultancy projects with industrial partners. If any industrial partner wants to engage with me for any kind of sponsored research or consultancy, then he or she should initiate the process by contacting me, and indicating the scope of the project. After that we can start a round of discussions, and if we have mutual agreement, then we can take up the issue formally with IIT Delhi.
